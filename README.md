# Tamil Nadu Election 2026 Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026 and compares it with the 2021 election results to identify major political shifts, constituency-level changes, regional voting patterns, reserved-seat trends, and electoral competitiveness.

The objective of this analysis is to transform raw election data into meaningful insights through interactive dashboards and data storytelling using Power BI.

---

## Business Problem

Tamil Nadu witnessed significant political changes in the 2026 Assembly Election. This project aims to answer the following questions:

1. How did seat distribution change across Tamil Nadu's six regions?
2. Which constituencies changed their winning party?
3. Did reserved constituencies behave differently from general constituencies?
4. How did the margin of victory change between 2021 and 2026?
5. What do these changes reveal about voter behavior and political realignment?

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Election Overview
Provides a high-level summary of:
- Total Constituencies
- Total Candidates
- Total Votes Polled
- Winning Party
- Winning Party Seats
- Flipped Constituencies

### 2. Geographic Story
Analyzes seat distribution shifts across:
- Chennai Metro
- North
- Central
- Kongu
- Delta
- South

### 3. Flip Story
Identifies constituencies that changed their winning party between 2021 and 2026.

Key findings include:
- More than 160 constituencies changed their winning party.
- TVK emerged as the largest beneficiary of constituency flips.
- Major seat transfers occurred from both DMK and AIADMK.

### 4. Reserved Seat Story
Compares electoral performance in:
- SC Reserved Constituencies
- ST Reserved Constituencies
- General Constituencies

### 5. Margin of Victory Story
Measures electoral competitiveness by comparing:
- Average Victory Margin (2021)
- Average Victory Margin (2026)

---

## Key Insights

### Geographic Story
- TVK emerged as a major force across all six regions.
- DMK lost seats in every region.
- Chennai Metro witnessed the largest political shift.
- Kongu and South Tamil Nadu became major TVK strongholds.

### Flip Story
- A large number of constituencies changed their winning party.
- TVK gained seats from both DMK and AIADMK.
- The election reflected substantial electoral volatility.

### Reserved Seat Story
- TVK performed strongly in SC-reserved constituencies.
- DMK and AIADMK both experienced significant declines in reserved seats.
- Similar trends were observed in general constituencies.

### Margin of Victory Story
- Average victory margins declined from approximately 23K votes in 2021 to 17K votes in 2026.
- Elections became more competitive across the state.

---

## Key Conclusion

The 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election marked a major political realignment. TVK successfully expanded across regions and constituency types, while traditional party strongholds weakened. The high number of constituency flips and declining victory margins indicate a more competitive and dynamic political landscape.
